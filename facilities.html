Hostel Facilities

Our hostel consists of 102 rooms housing two boarders each. Every room has 2 beds, 2 cupboards, 2 tables, and two chairs. The rooms are spacious and we have 24 hours electicity. Apart from the rooms, our hostel provides the following facilities.

Guest Waiting Room

Our hostel has a guest room, where the parents and relatives of the boarders wishing to meet their wards can sit and relax as they wait, as entry into the hostel is restricted.

Kitchen

Our hostel Kitchen is well equipped with all the essential amenities such as refrigerator, mixer grinder, sufficient number of gas outlets, cooking utensils, kitchen chimneys, large space, and a clean environment. The boarders are also allowed to use the gas outlets and water heating kettles for preparing snacks and beverages.

Dining Hall

We have a huge dining hall capable of seating 96 people at a time. The dining hall is always well maintained and the tables and chairs are kept neat and clean. The dining area is open for three meals a day, Breakfast(8:30-10:30 am), Lunch(12-2pm) and Dinner(8:30-10:30pm).

24*7 Internet Wifi

Our hostel has 24 hours internet facility. Internet connection is provided to the boarders wirelessly by means of Wi-Fi routers placed strategically at different locations within the hostel. All connections to the internet are through a proxy server which provides security as well as flexibility in maintenance.

Recreation Room

The recreation room in our hostel has a Table Tennis board, Carrom Board, and other board games. This is where the boarders can chill out, play and have fun.

Common Room

The hostel common room is where meetings and discussions (both formal and informal), hostel events such as Fresher's social, Farewell, and other functions and events are held. When there is no such event, the common room is used by the boarders to watch TV, read newspapers and magazines, or just sit and chat with friends. The common room is equipped with a satellite TV with set top box, and a music system.

Coffee Shop

We have a coffee shop within our hostel premises, which provides the boarders the odd time coffee, tea and snacks. We find it most useful during the exam times.
